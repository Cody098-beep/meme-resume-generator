About Me

We need unnecessary lore, mock seriousness, and weird confessions that make people think,
“I don’t know if this person’s a genius or just hasn’t slept in three days.”

Although there's nothing crazy about me but here's a little description about me:

So Hey, Everybody (with little awkwardness) I’m the human disaster behind this website. The kind of person who looked at a boring resume and thought:

“What if this had more Shrek?”

I’m not a software engineer. I’m not a designer. I’m not even sure I’m fully human at this point — I’m mostly caffeine and memes held together by anxiety and leftover pizza.

This all started when I was applying for jobs and realized my resume was so dry it could be used as kindling. I needed spice. I needed chaos. I needed something that would make a recruiter laugh so hard it Seemed like they had inhaled laughing gas.
And thus, Meme Resume Generator was born.
